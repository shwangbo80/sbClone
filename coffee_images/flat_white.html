Flat White

Smooth ristretto shots of espresso get the perfect amount of steamed whole milk to create a not-too-strong, not-too-creamy, just-right flavor.

Available sizes:


Short
(237mL)

Tall
(354mL)

Grande
(473mL)

Venti
(591mL)

Also available in the following milk options: Trim milk, Almond milk, Soy milk, Coconut milk, Oat milk.

Try a Flat White at your local Starbucks Store.
